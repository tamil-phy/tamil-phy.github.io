About Me

Hey there! I am Tamil Arasan Bakthavatchalam, a theoretical physicist turned machine learning aficionado. My roots trace back to the lively city of Pondicherry, where I first discovered my passion for uncovering the mysteries of the universe.

I initially pursued a career in engineering, but after realizing it wasn't my true calling, I decided to follow my passion for physics from scratch. This decision led me to the University of Madras, where I earned my Ph.D. and explored the fascinating dynamics of nonlinear systems.

Currently, I am diving deep into the world of AI at SAAMA, tackling clinical trial challenges with cutting-edge machine learning techniques.

I am always on the lookout for interesting conversations and collaboration opportunities. Shoot me an email if you'd like to chat about physics, AI, or anything in between!
